The csv contains medical information on Hypertrophic obstructive cardiomyopathy (HOCM) including symptoms such as sharp chest pain, difficulty breathing, anxiety and nervousness, chest tightness, leg swelling, joint pain, cough, emotional symptoms, elbow cramps or spasms, nailbiting, muscle swelling, and elbow weakness. The medical tests recommended include radiographic imaging procedure, echocardiography, ultrasonography (ultrasound), diagnostic ultrasound of heart (echocardiogram), cardiac stress tests, and psychotherapy, as well as a lipid panel. The recommended medications for treatment are Metoprolol, Verapamil (Univer), Trazodone, Testosterone, Buspirone (Buspar), Mirtazapine, Olmesartan (Benicar), Quetiapine (Seroquel), Lansoprazole (Prevacid), and Fluticasone Nasal Product.
